Guided tour of the quarries in the area and the ARJA factory

On this occasion we welcomed a visit from our distributor in Lebanon with their clients.

First of all we prepared an agenda adapting ourselves to the needs of the clients and introducing them to our facilities. To do this, the gravel pits and quarries are located where our crushers are working with material of the same characteristics that they are dealing with, in this case, limestone.

 

The first day was devoted to visiting stone crushing plants using the impact system. This visit allowed us to take a few samples so that we could analyse the quality of the product handled with our crushers. In addition we were also able to demonstrate the latest advances in applied automation in crushing systems with which we are working. A complete plant controlled by a single operator.

The second day focused on washing stations. Our selection of potential customers shared the need to increase the quality of their sands for the manufacture of concrete, reducing the percentage of filler.

 

If you would like to get to know us more closely, you are all welcome to Arja.
